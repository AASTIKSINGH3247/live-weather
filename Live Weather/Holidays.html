Significance of Abhyang Snan

Abhyang Snan during Narak Chaturdashi is an important purification ritual. It is believed to absolve sins and prevent suffering in the afterlife. Traditionally performed before sunrise using sesame oil and ubtan, it marks the beginning of Choti Diwali or Roop Chaturdashi.
